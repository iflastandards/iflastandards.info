Medo Winnie zvani Pooh (1986; Mladost; svezak; tvrdi uvez)

A single printed volume of a work of fiction translated from English into Croatian. The publisher is one of the two that have published Milne’s novel, and the translator is one of two translators. It is necessary to distinguish this manifestation from others published so far: string encoding schemes used for values of has authorized access point of manifestation and has expression embodied in manifestation reflect that. The manifestation is a sub-unit of a series that has been terminated. The translator changed her name, so the forms are different in values of has manifestation statement of title and responsibility and has person associated with manifestation, which is the authorized form of her name taken from the NUL, Croatia. The manifestation itself is an augmentation aggregate, with Milne’s text augmented by Shepard’s illustrations.

The focus of the description is the manifestation of the issue, related to the manifestation of the series as a super-unit. It is related to a description of the series as a whole, inversely related to the issue as a sub-unit, via authorized access points.
