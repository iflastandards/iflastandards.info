Absurd drama (1978; Penguin Books; volume; paperback)

A single printed volume of four plays, three of them translated into English, with an authored Introduction, 16 p. This is an aggregate manifestation that embodies four expressions and an augmenting expression. Translators’ names are provided in the Contents, a source of information for the manifestation itself, although not relevant as the information is recorded in a note. First published in Penguin Books in 1962 and reprinted several times, this manifestation is reprinted in 1978. The manifestation is a sub-unit of a series with no information about its termination.

The focus of the description is the manifestation and the super-unit publisher series is not described; it is a moot point to include a relationship to the series. A string encoding scheme for the has authorized access point of manifestation elemeent is specific for issues of a publisher series. The relationships are mediated in this example via authorized access points.
