Evangelistarium Marci Maruli Spalatensi (201-; Nacionalna i sveučilišna knjižnica u Zagrebu; online jedinica građe)

A digitised reproduction of a single older printed volume. The digitised reproduction is a sub-unit of a collection of digital manifestations "Digitalna zbirka djela Marka Marulića", which itself is a sub-unit of a collection "Zbirka rukopisa i starih knjiga NSK" (National and University Library's Collection of manuscripts and old books). The collection is not described in this example. The manifestation reproduces a specific item, including modifications such as annotations and visible wear of binding and pages. The language of cataloguing is Croatian.
