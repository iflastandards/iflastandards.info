Biblioteka Vjeverica (1957-1998, Mladost; volumen; tvrdi uvez)

A manifestation of a series that is issued as printed volumes. The series is considered to be terminated.

The focus of the description is the manifestation of the series, related to the manifestations of its issues as sub-units. It is related to a description of the issue, inversely related to the series as a super-unit, via authorized access points.
