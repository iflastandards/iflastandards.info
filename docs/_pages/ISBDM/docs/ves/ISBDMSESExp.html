Expression SES

This is a suggested string encoding scheme for authorized access points of expressions that are related to the manifestation.

The base access point of an expression is an authorized access point of the work that is realised by the expression.

The string encoding scheme specifies that the base access point is followed by a qualifier that is added to the qualifiers of the access point, if any, and separated by a semicolon "; ".

{authorized access point of the work] + insert before ")"("; " + {a category of content of the expression})

The qualifier supports a local secondary collocation and sort order of authorized access points of expressions in a browse index for expressions.

Other qualifiers are added to the sequence to disambiguate the basic qualified access point.

Added qualifiers are conditional on specific characteristics of the manifestation that are recorded as attribute elements. They support a tertiary collocation and sort order of authorized access points.

Examples

has expression embodied in manifestation
"The Beatles. Eleanor Rigby (performed music; 2022 stereo mix)"
has expression embodied in manifestation
"The Beatles. Eleanor Rigby (performed music; take 2)"
[Full example: Revolver (2022; Apple Corps Limited; audio disc; 2 CD edition). The auhorized access point of the work that is realized by the expression is qualified with a category of content and the version.]

Qualify a value of a category of content of the expression that is "spoken word", "tactile text", or "text" with a value of a language of the expression.

Examples

has expression embodied in manifestation
"McCall Smith, Alexander, 1948-. Emma (text; English)"
[Full example: Emma (2014 ; Borough Press; volume; case binding).]
has expression embodied in manifestation
"Poe, Edgar Allan, 1809-1849. Murders in the Rue Morgue (text; English)"
[Full example: Black Lizard big book of locked-room mysteries (2014; Vintage Books; volume; perfect binding).]
has expression embodied in manifestation
"Dib, Mohammed, 1920-2003, Incendie (texte; français)"
[Full example: Incendie (1954; Éditions du Seuil; volume; livre broché).]
has expression embodied in manifestation
"Rowling, J.K., 1965-. Harry Potter and the goblet of fire (spoken word; English)"
[Full example: Harry Potter and the goblet of fire (2000; Listening Library; audiocassette).]
