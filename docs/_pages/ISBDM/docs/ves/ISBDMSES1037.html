Compound title SES

This is a suggested string encoding scheme for a value of a has title of manifestation element that is a compound title.

For a manifestation that has a super-unit, the string encoding scheme combines values of strings that are taken from statements of title and responsibility that appear in the manifestation, in the following order:

  1. a common title of a super-unit of the manifestation
  2. a unit designation of the manifestation
  3. a unit title of the manifestation

The component strings are separate by a colon and space ": ".

The ISBDM string template is:

[common title] + ": " + [unit designation} + ": " + [unit title]

Examples

has title proper
"Homazh për De Radën: DVD 1"
[Full example: Homazh për De Radën: DVD 1 (2004; Università della Calabria; videodisk). The value uses a common title and a unit designation; there is no unit title.]
has variant title of manifestation
"New scientist. The collection: Vol three / issue three: The quantum world"
[Full example: Quantum world (2016; Reed Business Information Ltd; volume). The value uses a common title, a unit designation, and a unit title.]

For a manifestation that is an aggregate, the string encoding scheme combines values of strings that are titles of distinct expressions or works that are taken from statements of title and responsibility that appear in the manifestation, in the order of appearance.

The component strings are separate by a space, plus sign, and space " + ".

The ISBDM string template is:

[first title of expression] + " +: " + [second title of expression} + " + " + [third title of expression] …

Examples

has title proper
"Voyage extraordinaire au coeur de l’atome + Voyage extraordinaire aux limites de l’univers"
has title proper
"Voyage extraordinaire aux limites de l’univers + Voyage extraordinaire au coeur de l’atome"
Full example: Voyage extraordinaire au coeur de l’atome + Voyage extraordinaire aux limites de l’univers (1996; Editions Casterman; volume). The values use the distinct titles of expressions that are embodied in the manifestation in a tête-bêche layout.
