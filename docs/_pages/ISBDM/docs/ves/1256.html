ISBDM Manufacturing Process value vocabulary

This value vocabulary is a source of values for a has manufacturing process element.

Value
Definition
Scope note

automatic conversion

A manufacturing process that consists of a process in which the computer representation of a content is changed from one system to another by means of automatic algorithms or applications.

burning

A manufacturing process that consists of a process in which a laser beam etching a surface to produce pits readable as digital signals, such as in a CD-ROM or a DVD-ROM

daguerrotype process

A manufacturing process that consists of a photographic process using a silver-coated copper plate that is exposed in a camera and subsequently developed, usually using mercury vapour, and fixed with salt to create a positive image.

drawing

A manufacturing process that consists of representing something by lines made on a surface by means of a pencil or pen without the use of colour.

electroforming

A manufacturing process that consists of a process in which a metal layer is electrodeposited on a model, or mandrel, to manufacture pressing stampers.

electronic recording

A manufacturing process that consists of capturing data or information by means of a computer keyboard or other device and fixing for storage and manipulation in an automated system, and that requires the use of the system to render the data or information intelligible by a person.

embossing

A manufacturing process that consists of using either a metal or plastic sheet as a master with specialized equipment to produce copies for a manifestation of a tactile expression.

etching

A manufacturing process that consists of an intaglio process in which the design is worked into an acid-resistant substance coating the metal printing plate; the plate is then exposed to acid, which etches the plate where the metal is exposed, to create lines and dark areas.

handwriting

A manufacturing process that consists of writing by hand on a surface, by means of a pen, pencil, stylus, etc

hard copying

A manufacturing process that consists of a process in which a document that is stored on a computer is printed on a paper, transparent, or other surface.

impression

A manufacturing process that consists of pressing an object onto a surface to obtain a mark or outline.

magnetic recording

A manufacturing process that consists of storing sounds or moving pictures converted into electrical signals by means of changes in areas of magnetization on a tape, wire, or disc.

optical recording

A manufacturing process that consists of storing sounds or images converted into digital or analogue signals by means of changes in areas of a reflecting surface, so that they may be captured by an optical sensor.

photocopying

A manufacturing process consisting of a macroform photoreproduction process using radiant energy in contact with or projected on opaque material.

photogravure process

A manufacturing process that consists of an intaglio process in which the metal printing plate is prepared using a dichromate process to leave a gelatin resist coating of varying thickness that is etched to form cells of varying depth able to hold different amounts of ink.

pressing

A manufacturing process that consists of a process in which a machine flattens, compresses, and squeezes materials to a predetermined shape such as an LP record or a CD.

stamping

A manufacturing process that consists of the application of pressure to make an impression on the surface of a material.

typing

A manufacturing process that consists of producing text or other content by means of a computer keyboard or typewriter.
