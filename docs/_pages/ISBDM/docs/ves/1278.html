ISBDM Extent of Aggregated Content value vocabulary

This value vocabulary is a source of values for a has extent of aggregated content element.

The vocabulary does not cover the full scope of the element and may be extended with additional values.

Value
Definition
Scope note

chart

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a still image that is a representation of data in graphic or tabular form.

diagram

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a still image that is a representation of numeric data, or of an action or process.

drawing

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a still image that is composed of lines that represent the delineation of form.

icon

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a still image that portrays a sacred entity and that is itself regarded as sacred.

map

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a cartographic image that represents an area on the surface of the earth, another celestial body, or an imaginary place.

part

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is notated music.

performed dance

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is performed movement that is recorded in a live performance.

performed song

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is performed music that is recorded in a live performance.

photograph

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a still image that is captured by a lens.

profile

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a cartographic image.

radiograph

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a still image produced by radiation.

recorded song

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is performed music that is recorded in a studio.

remote-sensing image

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a cartographic image that is captured by a remote-sensing instrument.

score

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is notated music.

section

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a cartographic image that represents a slice through the surface of the earth or some other celestial body.

spoken text

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is spoken word that is a recitation of a text.

technical drawing

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a still image that represents a cross section of an object or part of an object for use in an engineering or other technical context.

view

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is a still image that represents a landscape from an oblique point of view.

A bird's-eye view, panorama, panoramic drawing, worm's-eye view, etc., are included.

vocal score

A unit of extent of aggregated content that is notated music.
