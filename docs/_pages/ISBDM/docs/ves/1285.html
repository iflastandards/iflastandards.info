ISBDM Designation of Sound Channels value vocabulary

This value vocabulary is a source of values for a has designation of number of sound channels element.

Value
Definition
Scope note

mono

A designation of sound channels that is a single channel for monophonic sound.

quadraphonic

A designation of sound channels that is four channels for quadraphonic sound.

stereo

A designation of sound channels that is two channels for stereophonic sound.

surround

A designation of sound channels that is more than four channels for surround sound.
