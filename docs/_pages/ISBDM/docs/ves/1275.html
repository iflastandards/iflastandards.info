ISBDM Extent of Unitary Structure value vocabulary

This value vocabulary is a source of values for a has extent of unitary structure element.

The vocabulary does not cover the full scope of the element and may be extended with additional values.

Value
Definition
Scope note

activity card

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a card to be used as a basis for performing a specific activity.

An activity card may be part of a set of cards, and usually embodies a text or a still image.

coin

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object that is a piece of metal stamped by government authority for use as money.

collage

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a sheet on which is affixed paper, textiles, or other two-dimensional materials.

A collage usually embodies a still image.

diorama

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object in which miniature scenery, figurines, and other components are placed in front of a background sheet to embody a three-dimensional form.

exhibit

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object ithat consists of other objects gathered together for display.

An exhibit may include display cases, captions, and panels that give information about the gathered objects.

flash card

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a card that is used for rapid display as an aid to learning.

game

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object that consists of a group of other objects that are manipulated according to a set of rules for purposes of education, entertainment, or therapy.

globe

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object that is a sphere.

A globe embodies a cartographic image on its surface.

jigsaw puzzle

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a sheet that is cut into interlocking shapes for reassembly.

A jigsaw puzzle usually embodies a still image.

medal

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object that is a piece of metal that is used to commemorate something.

mock-up

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object that is a scaled-down representation of a part of another object that may be modified for training or analysis to emphasize a particular part or function.

model

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object that is a scaled-down representation of another object.

painting

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a sheet on which pigment is applied by hand.

A painting embodies a still image.

piece

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a sheet or object that is intended for manipulation as a component of a game or puzzle.

postcard

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a card that is used for sending a message without an envelope via a mail service.

A postcard may embody a text or a still image.

poster

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a sheet that is used to publicize an event, product, or service, or as a decorative wall hanging.

A poster may embody a text or a still image

print

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a sheet that is used for a high-quality reproduction.

A print embodies a still image.

sculpture

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object made of material that is worked into a shape.

A sculpture embodies a three-dimensional image.

specimen

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object tthat is intended to be representative of a larger group of similar objects.

study print

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a sheet that is used for education.

A study print embodies a still image that may be augmented with a text.

toy

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is an object that is intended for education, entertainment, or stimulation through play.

wall chart

A unit of extent of unitary structure that is a sheet that is intended for display on a wall.
