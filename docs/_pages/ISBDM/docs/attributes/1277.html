has extent of embodied content

Element reference

Definition
Relates a manifestation to an extent of manifestation that is a measurement of the layout of content that is embodied in the manifestation.
Scope note
Domain
Manifestation
Range
Element sub-type
has designation of number of sound channels has image resolution has number of sound channels has playing time has reduction ratio
Element super-type
has extent of manifestation

Additional information

See also: has layout of embodied content

Element values

For an extent of a cartographic image, cartographic tactile image, still image, or tactile image, round a fraction of the unit of length to the nearest whole number.

Record a has note on physical characteristic element that gives a more precise measurement, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata.

Examples

has extent of embodied content
"60 cm × 81 cm"
has note on physical characteristic
"Dimensions of still image: 60.33 cm × 81.12 cm"
[Full example: Treason of images (1929; Magritte, René, 1898-1967; sheet).]

Stipulations

Use the element super-type to record a less specific attribute.

Use an appropriate element sub-type to record a more specific attribute.

Examples

has number of sound channels
"6 channels"
[Full example: Lady Bird (2018; Universal Pictures Home Entertainment; videodisc; BD).]
has playing time
"49 min 14 sec"
[Full example: Stax uncovered! (2023; H Bauer Publishing Ltd; audio disc).]

Apply the stipulations for the element super-type unless indicated otherwise.

Record a quantitative value that consists of a whole number and a controlled term that appears in the ISBDM Layout of Embodied Content value vocabulary, in the language or script of choice of the cataloguing agency.

Examples

has extent of embodied content
"44 pages"
[Full example: Definitive guide to PSM 1 (2021; Knüeppel, Moritz; volume; perfect binding).]

For a manifestation that is an aperture card, film roll, filmslip, filmstrip, filmstrip cartridge, microfiche, microfiche cassette, microfilm cartridge, microfilm cassette, microfilm reel, microfilm roll, microfilm slip, microopaque, or stereograph disc, record the number of frames of content. Record a quantitative value that consists of a whole number and the term "frame" that appears in the ISBDM Layout of Embodied Content value vocabulary, in the language or script of choice of the cataloguing agency.

Examples

has extent of embodied content
"12 frames"

See also: has frame alignment

For a manifestation that is a card, sheet, or volume, record the total number of pages of content. Record a quantitative value that consists of a whole number and the term "page" that appears in the ISBDM Layout of Embodied Content value vocabulary, in the language or script of choice of the cataloguing agency.

Examples

has extent of embodied content
"256 pages"
[Full example: Secret library (2016; Michael O’Mara Books Limited; volume; case binding).]
has extent of embodied content
"322 stron"
[Full example: Pałac papieski na Watykanie od końca V do początku XVI wieku (1999; Wydawnictwo Naukowe Uniwersytetu im. Adama Mickiewicza; wolumin). The language of cataloguing is Polish.]

For a manifestation that is a computer card, computer chip cartridge, computer disc, computer disc cartridge, computer tape cartridge, computer tape cassette, computer tape reel, or online resource, record the number of bytes of content. Record a quantitative value that consists of a whole number and a controlled term or standard symbol for a unit of binary data.

Examples

has extent of embodied content
"912 KB"
[Full example: Emma (1994; Project Gutenberg; online resource; plain text format).]
has extent of embodied content
"638 KB"
[Full example: Corso delli Fiumi Drino e Boiana nella Dalmatia (Bibliothèque nationale de France; online resource; PDF).]

For a manifestation that embodies a cartographic image, cartographic tactile image, still image, or tactile image, record the height and width of the image in whole numbers of the same unit of length. Record the number for the height followed by "x" followed by the number for the width, followed by a controlled term or standard symbol for the unit of length.

Examples

has extent of embodied content
"70 x 50 cm"
[Full example: Zadar does Banksy (2023; Narodni muzej Zadar; list). The value is for an image that is the same size as one side of sheet.]
has extent of embodied content
"40 x 48 cm"
[Full example: Desserte rouge 1908 (1993; Editions du Centre Pompidou; sheet). The value is for an image that is smaller than one side of a sheet.]
has extent of embodied content
"94 x 164 cm"
[Full example: Loch Morar & Mallaig (2015; Ordnance Survey Ltd; cartographic image). The value is for an image that is larger than one side but smaller than both sides of a sheet.]
has extent of embodied content
"10 x 14 cm"
[Full example: Truman Capote, ecrivain, USA, 1947 (2008; Nouvelles Images S.A.; card). The value is for an image that is smaller than one side of a card.]
