Entity-relationship diagram of an augmented expression aggregate without a collective title

The diagram shows the case when the aggregating work that is embodied in an augmented expression aggregate is not specifically identified. The aggregated augmented expression is described as a separate related expression. There is a choice of which of the other aggregated augmenting expressions or their works to describe as separate related expressions and works, or the aggregating work to describe as a separate related work, and which to include in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. Optional entities, manifestation statements, and notes are indicated by dashes.
