Entity-relationship diagram of the manifestation of a diachronic work as a whole

The diagram shows the diachronic work that is described as a separate related work. There is a choice to describe the manifestations of the issues of the diachronic work as separate related manifestations, or to include the issue work or its manifestation in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. Optional entities, manifestation statements, and notes are indicated by dashes.
