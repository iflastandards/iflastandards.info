has place of creation of manifestation

Element reference

Definition
Relates a manifestation to a place in which the manifestation is manufactured, produced, or published.
Scope note
Domain
Manifestation
Range
Place
Element sub-type
has place of manufacture has place of production has place of publication
Element super-type
has place associated with manifestation

Additional information

Consider a manifestation that has a value of category of carrier of "online resource" to be published.

See also: has location of collection

Stipulations

✽

If one or more places of creation are associated with the manifestation, record at least one occurrence of the element. Use the following order of preference to select the value:

  1. Place of publication of manifestation
  2. Place of production of manifestation
  3. Place of manufacture of manifestation

Examples

has place of creation of manifestation
http://www.wikidata.org/entity/Q84
[Full example: Who I am (2012; HarperCollinsPublishers; volume; case binding). IRI of place of publication.]
has place of creation of manifestation
"London (England)"
[Full example: Catalogue of shipwrecked books (2018; William Collins; volume; case binding). The value is an authorized access point of the place of publication.]

Record additional occurrences of the element, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata.

Use the element super-type to record a less specific relationship.

Use an appropriate element sub-type to record a more specific relationship.

Examples

has place of publication
"London (England)"
[Full example: Charles Rennie Mackintosh (2002; Thames & Hudson; volume; perfect binding). The associated place is the city where the manifestation is published.]
has place of production
"Zagreb (Croatia)"
[Full example: Pogled na vrt Akademije (195-; Mujadžić, Omer, 1903-1991; sheet). The associated place is the city where the manifestation is produced.]
has place of manufacture
"Basel (Švicarska)"
[Full example: Evangelistarium Marci Maruli Spalatensis (1519; Officina Adamae Petri; svezak). The associated place is the city where the manifestation is printed.]

Apply the stipulations for the element super-type unless indicated otherwise.
