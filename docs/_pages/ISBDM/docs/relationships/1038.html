has title proper

Element reference

Definition
Relates a manifestation to a nomen that is a title of the manifestation that is selected for preference.
Scope note
Domain
Manifestation
Range
Nomen
Element sub-type
Element super-type
has title of manifestation

Additional information

A title proper is selected as the chief name of the manifestation from all of the titles that appear in the manifestation and external sources of information.

A title proper is normally the basis of the value of a has authorized access point of manifestation element.

Stipulations

Use the element super-type to record a less specific relationship.

Apply the stipulations for the element super-type unless indicated otherwise.

Record a value that is selected from one or more values of the element super-type. Use the following order of preference:

  1. The title that is made most prominent by typography in the manifestation.
  2. The title that is the fullest.
  3. The title that is in the same language or script as the spoken word, tactile text, or text content that is embodied in the manifestation.
  4. The title that is given in the language or script of choice of the cataloguing agency.

Record a value that includes a name of an agent or a title of edition that is integrated linguistically with a title that appears in a statement of title and responsibility.

Examples

has title proper
"Report of the Expert Group on Special Care for Babies"
has title proper
"The post-humous works of Robert Hooke"
has title proper
"Charles Aznavour présente ses plus grands succès"
has title proper
"The compact edition of the Oxford English dictionary"
has title proper
"鲁迅全集"

For a manifestation that embodies cartographic moving image, moving image, performed movement, or three-dimensional moving image content, record a value that omits a name that is integrated linguistically with a title that appears in a statement of title and responsibility and that is the name of an agent who is associated with the creation of the content.

Examples

has title proper
"Star wars"
has title proper
"Thief"

For a manifestation that embodies notated music, performed music, or tactile notated music content, and if there is no distinctive title in the statement, record a value that includes a type of composition and the key, numbering, date of composition, or medium of performance that appear in a statement of title and responsibility.

Examples

has title proper
"String quintet no. 1, A major, op. 18"
has title proper
"Sinfonia 1 (1970)"
has title proper
"降B大调第一弦乐五重奏, 作品第K174号"

For a manifestation that has a media type of "computer", and if no title appears in the manifestation or external sources of information, record the filename.

Examples

has title of manifestation
"notes23.txt"

For a manifestation that has a super-unit, and if no specific title of the unit appears in any statement of title and responsibility or external source of information, or if the specific title is dependent on a common title of the super-unit, record a value that is a compound title.

Examples

has title proper
"Dick’s picks, volume one, Tampa Florida 12/19/73"
[Title of the sub-unit is the place and date of a recorded music performance.]

For a manifestation that is an aggregate, record a value that is a collective title that appears in a statement of title and responsibility.

Examples

has title proper
"Three notable stories"
has title proper
"New poems"

For a manifestation that is an aggregate, and if no collective title appears in any statement of title and responsibility or external source of information, record a distinct value of the title that appears first in a statement of title and responsibility.

For a manifestation that embodies the expression of a diachronic work, record a value that omits designations of issue or iteration from a common title that appears in statements of title and responsibility in manifestations that embody issues or iterations of the work.

Examples

has title proper
"Report on the … Conference on Development Objectives and Strategy"
has title proper
"La sidérurgie française en …"

For a manifestation that embodies an expression of an issue or iteration of a diachronic work, record a value that includes designations of issue or iteration in a common title that appears in a statement of title and responsibility.

Examples

has title proper
"Report on the 2nd Conference on Development Objectives and Strategy"
has title proper
"Orkney archaeology review 2021"
