has title of manifestation

Element reference

Definition
Relates a manifestation to a nomen of the manifestation that is an uncontrolled label.
Scope note
This element includes a title proper, a variant title, a collective title, a common title, a compound title, and a title of a sub-unit.
Domain
Manifestation
Range
Nomen
Element sub-type
has title proper has variant title of manifestation
Element super-type
has appellation of manifestation

Additional information

A title of a manifestation is assigned by an agent who creates the manifestation or who creates works and expressions that are embodied in the manifestation.

Consider a title that is assigned by an agent who owns the only item that exemplifies the manifestation to be a title of the item and not a title of the manifestation.

A title of manifestation may have one or more of the following characteristics:

  • A title may be the same string as the name or title of another manifestation or of another entity.
  • A title may be a transliteration by the cataloguing agency from a script that cannot be transcribed.
  • A title may be given in different languages and scripts in statements that appear in the manifestation or in external sources. Consider each distinct value as a separate title.
  • A title may consist of or include an initialism or a transcription or transliteration of a logo or other image.
  • A title may include numbers, punctuation marks, and symbols.
  • A title may include a statement of responsibility or edition that is integrated linguistically with the title.
  • A title may reflect the characteristics of expressions or works that are embodied in the manifestation.

Element values

The value of a title is a literal string (a nomen string).

Record a value that is:

  • a title that appears in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation
  • a title that appears in a statement of title and responsibility that is reproduced in the manifestation
  • a title that is associated with the manifestation in an external source of information

Stipulations

Use the element super-type to record a less specific relationship.

Use an appropriate element sub-type to record a more specific relationship.

Apply the stipulations for the element super-type unless indicated otherwise.

Record a single occurrence of the element that is selected from one or more values that are associated with the manifestation. Use the following order of preference:

  1. Nomen that is a title proper of the manifestation
  2. Nomen that is a variant title of the manifestation

Record additional occurrences of the element, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata.

Record a value with the same wording as it appears in a statement of title and responsibility in the manifestation. Record a value with minor amendments to capitalisation and punctuation, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata.

Record a value that includes a name of an agent or a title of edition that is integrated linguistically with a title that appears in a statement of title and responsibility.

For a manifestation that is an aggregate, and if no collective title appears in any statement of title and responsibility or external source of information, record a value that is a compound title that combines the titles of expressions or works that appear together in a statement of title and responsibility. Combine the titles in the order of layout in the statement. Include words and phrases that conjoin the titles or indicate an edition that is specific to a title, but exclude statements of responsibility. Do not indicate omissions from the statement. Separate the titles with standard punctuation that is chosen by the cataloguing agency, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata. For titles from statements that differ in prominence in the typography, combine the titles in the order of prominence, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata.

Examples

has title of manifestation
"Flash and filigree & The magic Christian"
[Taken from a direct transcription of the statement.]
has title of manifestation
"Flash and filigree ; and, The magic Christian"
[Taken from a transcription of the statement with added and substituted punctuation.]

For a manifestation that is an aggregate, and if no collective title appears in any statement of title and responsibility or external source of information, and if a large number of titles of expressions or works appear together in a statement of title and responsibility, record a value that is a compound title that combines the first few titles in the order of layout in the most prominent statement. Include words and phrases that conjoin the titles or indicate an edition that is specific to a title, but exclude statements of responsibility. Do not indicate omissions from the statement, but follow the compound title with an ellipsis. Separate the titles with standard punctuation that is chosen by the cataloguing agency, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata.

Examples

has title of manifestation
"Electronic writing ; Functional grammar ; Verbal communication …"

For a manifestation that is an aggregate, and if no collective title appears in any statement of title and responsibility or external source of information, record a distinct value for one or more titles of expressions or works that appear together in a statement of title and responsibility, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata.

Examples

has title of manifestation
"Flash and filigree"
has title of manifestation
"The magic Christian"
