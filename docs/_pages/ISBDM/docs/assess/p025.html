Manifestation that embodies augmented or collected expressions

For a manifestation that is an augmented expression aggregate or a collected expressions aggregate, and if a collective title is given in a statement of title and responsibility, record a has work embodied in manifestation element for the aggregating work that is embodied in the manifestation.

Examples

has work embodied in manifestation
"Greenberg, Jane. Knitting the Semantic Web"
[Full example: Knitting the Semantic Web (2007; Howarth Information Press; volume). The manifestation is a collected expressions aggregate with a collective title]
has work embodied in manifestation
"Moffat, Alistair, 1950-. The faded map"
[Full example: Faded map (2010; Birlinn Limited; volume; case binding). The manifestation is an augmented expression aggregate with a collective title for the augmented work and augmenting works.]

Figure 6 shows the case when the collective title identifies the aggregating work that is embodied in an augmented expression aggregate or a collected expressions aggregate. In general, the aggregating work is described as a separate related work. This satisfies the mandatory requirement for a has work embodied in manifestation element. There is a choice of which of the aggregated expressions or their works to describe as separate related expressions and works, and which to include in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. The options for entities, manifestation statements, and notes are outlined by dashes.

For a manifestation that is an augmented expression aggregate, and if a collective title is not given in a statement of title and responsibility, record a has expression embodied in manifestation or a has work embodied in manifestation element for the augmented expression or work.

Figure 7 shows the case when the aggregating work that is embodied in an augmented expression aggregate is not specifically identified. In general, the aggregated augmented expression or its work is described as a separate related expression or work. This satisfies the mandatory requirement for a has expression embodied in manifestation element or a has work embodied in manifestation element. There is a choice of which of the other aggregated augmenting expressions or their works to describe as separate related expressions and works, or of the aggregating work to describe as a separate related work, and which to include in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. The options for entities, manifestation statements, and notes are outlined by dashes.

For a manifestation that is a collected expressions aggregate, and if a collective title is not given in a statement of title and responsibility, record a has expression embodied in manifestation or a has work embodied in manifestation element for one of the expressions or works that are aggregated. Use the following order of preference to select the expression or work:

  1. The first expression or work that is named and given prominence in the typography and layout of a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation.
  2. The first expression or work that is named in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation.

Figure 8 shows the case when the aggregating work that is embodied in a collected expressions aggregate is not specifically identified. In general, one of the aggregated collected expressions or their works is selected to be described as a separate related expression or work. This satisfies the mandatory requirement for a has expression embodied in manifestation elementor a has work embodied in manifestation element. There is a choice of which of the other aggregated collected expressions or their works to describe as separate related expressions and works, or the aggregating work to describe as a separate related work, and which to include in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. The options for entities, manifestation statements, and notes are outlined by dashes.

For a manifestation that is an augmented expression aggregate or a collected expressions aggregate, record additional occurrences of has expression embodied in manifestation or has work embodied in manifestation elements for expressions or works that are aggregated, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata. Use the following order of preference to select the expressions or works:

  1. The augmented expression that is embodied in an augmented expression aggregate.
  2. Expressions that are named and given prominence in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation.
  3. Expressions that are named in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation.
  4. Expressions that are named in other statements that appear in the manifestation.
  5. Expressions that are identified in the manifestation or in external sources of information.
  6. Works that are realised by the expressions that are aggregated.
