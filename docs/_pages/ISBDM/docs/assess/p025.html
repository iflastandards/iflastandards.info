Describing the manifestation

Manifestation that embodies augmented or collected expressions

If a collective title is given in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in an augmented expression aggregate or a collected expressions aggregate, record a has work embodied in manifestation element for the aggregating work.

Examples

has work embodied in manifestation
"Greenberg, Jane. Knitting the Semantic Web"
[Full example: Knitting the Semantic Web (2007; Howarth Information Press; volume).]

Record a has work embodied in manifestation element for the work that is realised by the expressions that are aggregated in a parallel expressions aggregate.

If a collective title is not given in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in an augmented expression aggregate or a collected expressions aggregate, record a has work embodied in manifestation element for one of the expressions that are aggregated. Use the following order of preference to select the expression:

  1. The first expression that is named and given prominence in the typography and layout of a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation.
  2. The first expression that is named in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation.

Record additional occurrences of has expression embodied in manifestation or has work embodied in manifestation elements for expressions that are aggregated, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata. Use the following order of preference to select the expressions:

  1. The augmented expression that is embodied in an augmented expression aggregate.
  2. Expressions that are named and given prominence in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation.
  3. Expressions that are named in a statement of title and responsibility that appears in the manifestation.
  4. Expressions that are named in other statements that appear in the manifestation.
  5. Expressions that are identified in the manifestation or in external sources of information.
  6. Works that are realised by the expressions that are aggregated.

Figure 6 shows the case when the collective title identifies the aggregating work that is embodied in an augmented expression aggregate or a collected expressions aggregate. In general, the aggregating work is described as a separate related work. This satisfies the mandatory requirement for a has work embodied in manifestation element. There is a choice of which of the aggregated expressions or their works to describe as separate related expressions and works, and which to include in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. The options for entities, manifestation statements, and notes are outlined by dashes.

Figure 7 shows the case when the aggregating work that is embodied in a collected expressions aggregate is not specifically identified. In general, one of the aggregated collected expressions is selected to be described as a separate related expression. This satisfies the mandatory requirement for a has expression embodied in manifestation element. There is a choice of which of the other aggregated collected expressions or their works to describe as separate related expressions and works, or the aggregating work to describe as a separate related work, and which to include in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. The options for entities, manifestation statements, and notes are outlined by dashes.

Figure 8 shows the case when the aggregating work that is embodied in an augmented expression aggregate is not specifically identified. In general, the aggregated augmented expression is described as a separate related expression. This satisfies the mandatory requirement for a has expression embodied in manifestation element. There is a choice of which of the other aggregated augmenting expressions or their works to describe as separate related expressions and works, or the aggregating work to describe as a separate related work, and which to include in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. The options for entities, manifestation statements, and notes are outlined by dashes.
