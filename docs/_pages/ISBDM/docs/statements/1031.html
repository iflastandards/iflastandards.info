has manifestation statement of issue or iteration

Element reference

Definition
Relates a manifestation to a manifestation statement that indicates that the manifestation embodies an issue, iteration, or unit of a diachronic work, and the names, functions, and other details of agents who are responsible for the creation of the diachronic work.
Scope note
This element includes a statement of the title, identifiers, and responsibility of the diachronic work and designations of the issue, iteration, or unit.

Manifestation
