has manifestation statement of edition

Element reference

Definition
Relates a manifestation to a manifestation statement that indicates that the manifestation is a version of another manifestation, and the names, functions, and other details of agents who are responsible for the creation of works and expressions that are embodied in the version.
Scope note
This element includes editions and drafts, presentation and layout versions, and stages in the manufacture of another manifestation.
Domain
Manifestation
Range
Literal
Element sub-type
Element super-type
has manifestation statement

Additional information

Consider a statement that includes the terms "edition", "issue", "printing", etc. and their equivalents in other languages to be an insufficient indication of a statement of edition, unless the term is accompanied by a qualification such as a number or a word or phrase that indicates a difference from other editions.

For a manifestation that embodies notated movement or notated music content, consider a statement that includes the layout or form of presentation of the notation to be an indication of a statement of edition.

See also: has manifestation associated with manifestation

See also: has note on manifestation statement of edition

Stipulations

Use the element super-type to record a less specific statement.

Apply the stipulations for the element super-type unless indicated otherwise.

has manifestation statement of edition
"Naslov originala, Alan Alexander Milne, Winnie-the-Pooh, Printed in Great Britain by Richard Clay (The Chaucer Press) Ltd., Bungay, Suffolk, Prvo izdanje objavljeno 1926 godine"
has manifestation statement of edition
"Prvo izdanje u ovoj biblioteci"
[Full example: Medo Winnie zvani Pooh (1986; Mladost; svezak). Each statement concerns a different edition.]
has manifestation statement of edition
"… Huile sur toile, 180 x 220 cm - Musée de l'Ermitage, Saint-Pétersbourg"
[Full example: Desserte rouge 1908 (1993; Editions du Centre Pompidou; sheet). The statement refers to the original painting that is reproduced in the manifestation.]
has manifestation statement of edition
"… co-published simultaneously as Cataloging & classification quarterly, volume 43, numbers 3/4 2007"
[Full example: Knitting the Semantic Web (2007; Howarth Information Press; volume). The statement refers to a separate manifestation that embodies an issue of a diachroic work.]

Record a has note on manifestation statement of edition element that gives information about a statement of edition that does not apply to the manifestation as a whole.
