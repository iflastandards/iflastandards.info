has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability

Element reference

Definition
Relates a manifestation to a statement that includes identifiers of the manifestation and general information about obtaining items that exemplify the manifestation.
Scope note
This element includes identifiers such as key titles, standard numbers, catalogue numbers, plate numbers, and chronological and numeric designations. Terms of availability include limitations on the number of exemplars, the cost, or an indication that there is no cost, of purchasing or borrowing an exemplar, and the categories of agent to whom the terms apply.
Domain
Manifestation

Additional information

For a manifestation that embodies the expression of a diachronic work, consider a statement about the frequency and pattern of issues to be a publication statement and not terms of availability

See also: has manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution

See also: has identifier of manifestation

See also: has terms of availability

See also: has note on manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability

Stipulations

Use the element super-type to record a less specific statement.

Apply the stipulations for the element super-type unless indicated otherwise.

Examples

has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability
"Not for resale."
[Full example: Stax uncovered! (2023; H Bauer Publishing Ltd; audio disc).]

Record a value that includes a qualifier to an identifier.

Examples

has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability

Record a value that includes more than one identifier or terms of availability that are included in a single statement that appears in the manifestation.

Examples

has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability
"ISBN 978-1-78330-185-6 (paperback), ISBN 978-1-78330-186-7 (hardback), ISBN 978-1-783-250-5 (e-book)"
[Full example: Open licensing for cultural heritage (2017; Facet Publishing; volume; perfect binding).]

Record a value that includes a statement about the limited availability of items that exemplify the manifestation, including small numbers of items and restrictions on distribution.

Examples

has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability
"Il a été tiré 15 exemplaires sur alfa cellunaf numéroté de 1 a 15 dont 5 hors commerce constituant l'édition originale"
[Full example: Incendie (1954; Éditions du Seuil; volume; livre broché).]

For a manifestation that embodies performed music, sounds, spoken word, performed movement, or two-dimensional moving image content, record a value that includes a label name that is associated with the catalogue number.

Examples

has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability
"AMMP CD0209"

If intermediary identifiers can be inferred, record a value that omits intermediary identifiers from a set of sequentially numbered identifiers.

Examples

has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability
"ART220BD/1 … 8"
has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability
"8 disks are numbered sequentially."
