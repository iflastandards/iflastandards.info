Examples of transcription

These examples illustrate the application of the Rules for transcribing a manifestation statement to the value of a has manifestation statement element or element sub-type.

has manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution
"London: Printed for John Murray. 1816"
[Full example: Emma (1815; John Murray; volume). The value transcribes the punctuation of the statement as it appears in the manifestation. Whitespace is not replaced by punctuation.]
has manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution
"First published in the United Kingdom in 2021 by Thames & Hudson … London …"
[Full example: Spring cannot be cancelled (2022; Thames & Hudson; volume). The value omits the address and postal code that are considered to be unnecessary detail, and replaces them with marks of omission "…".]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"Corso delli Fiumi Drino e Boiana nella Dalmatia / descritto dal P. Coronelli, cosmografo della Seren. Republica di [...]"
[Full example: Corso delli Fiumi Drino e Boiana nella Dalmatia (2007; Bibliothèque nationale de France; online resource; PDF). The value retains the punctuation of the statement. The mark of omission "..." in brackets is transcribed from the statement. It does not indicate an omission in transcription, and it is not encoded as an ellipsis.]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"Matisse Bonnard, Long live painting!"
[Full example: Matisse Bonnard (2017; Städel Museum; volume). The value interprets the layout and typography to include a space between the first two words. Another value could interpret the layout to include whitespace, and may replace it with clarifying punctuation.]
has manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution
"©2022 Aimee Stwart"
[Full example: Library (2022; Corner Piece Puzzles; object). The value includes a misprint; the letter "e" is missing from "Stwart".]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"Corso delli fiumi Drino, e Boiana nella Dalmatia, descrito dal P. Mro Coronelli cosmografo della Seren. Republica di Venezia. Dedicato agli Em:, e Reu:, signori cardinali della Sacra Congregatione de Propaganda Fide"
[Full example: Corso delli fiumi Drino, e Boiana nella Dalmatia (1688; fletë). The value retains abbreviations and does not expand them. The value replaces whitespace with the punctuation ", " (comma-space) for clarity.]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"Relacion verda dera, y breve de la persecvcion, y Martirios que padecieron por la confelsion de nueftra Santa Fee Catholica en Lapon, quinze, Reliogiofor de la provincia de S. Gregorio, de los Defcalcos del Orden de nuestro Seraphico P. S. Francifco de las Islas Philipinas = A brief and true accountof the persecution and martyrdomendured by fifteen Religious of the Provincia de San Gregorio de los Descalzos of the Franciscan Order in the Philippines for professing our holy faith in Japan"
[Full example: Relacion verda dera y breve de la persecucion y mortirios que padecieron por la confession de nuetra Santa Fee Catholica en Japon (2022; National Library of the Philippines; online resource). The value retains misprints that appear in the statement in the manifestation.]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"Johann Sebastian Bach; Was mir behagt, ist nur die muntre Jagd …; BWV 208; Mit der Sinfonia aus BWV 1046a und dem Instrumentalsatz BWV 1040 …; Jagdkantate; für Soli SSTB, Chor SS(A)TB; 2 Blockflöten, 2 Oboen, Taille (Sinfonia: 3 Oboen), Fagott, 2 Hörner; 2 Violinen, Viola, Violone und Basso continuo; von Ulrich Leisinger"
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"Johann Sebastian Bach; … The merry chase, the hunt is my delight; BWV 208; … includes the Sinfonia from BWV 1046a and the instrumental movement BWV 1040; Hunting Cantata; for soli SSTB, choir SS(A)TB; 2 recorders, 2 oboes, taille (Sinfonia: 3 oboes), bassoon, 2 horns; 2 violins, viola, violone and bassoon continuo; edited by Ulrich Leisinger; English version by Henry S. Drinker"
[Full example: Was mir behagt, ist nur die muntre Jagd (2021; Carus; volume; full score). "Johann Sebastian Bach" and "BWV 208" each appear once in the source of information and are included in the transcription of the parallel German and English statements. The mark of omission (…) indicates where the statements intersect.]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"More ABBA gold, more ABBA hits"
[The first “B” in “ABBA” is turned in the source of information. It is transcribed in its correct orientation.]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"myScience Canada"
[Full example: myScience Canada (2008-; Scimetrica; online resource). “Canada” is represented by an image of a flag.]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"voyage extraordinaire AU COEUR DE L’ATOME"
[Full example: Voyage extraordinaire au coeur de l’atome + Voyage extraordinaire aux limites de l’univers (1996; Editions Casterman; volume). The value retains the capitalization of the statement.]
has manifestation statement of title and responsibility
"lcd soundsystem | a. written by james murphy b. by james murphy and pat mahoney | a give it up | james murphy: vocals, bass, drums, percussion, synthesizers, programming | produce by the dfa | b tired | james murphy: vocals, bass, guitar; pat mahoney: drums, percussion; ems synthesizer | produce by the dfa"
[Full example: Give it up b/w Tired (2002; Ouput Recordings; audio disc). The value replaces whitespace with the punctuation " | " (space-vertical bar-space) for clarity. The value retains the capitalization of the statement.]
has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability
"ISSN:2800-0552(Print), 2951-116X(Online)"
[Full example: Journal of construction automation and robotics (2022-; Korean Society of Automation and Robotics in Construction; online resource). The value retains the spacing of the statement.]
